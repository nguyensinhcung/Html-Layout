Alpha Petto Shells

Alpha Petto Shells (APS) are an ERC 20 utility token of the Pettoverse. Holders will participate in the decision-making of the future of Petto supporting game evolution and deciding on the allocation of a community treasury.

What is APS→What is APS→What is APS→What is APS→What is APS→ What is APS→

APS is the native token across the Pettoverse. APS can only be earned within the Pettoverse and it’s crucial to the entire game experience and benefits.

APS has a capped total supply and can be exchanged on decentralized marketplaces. Also, APS is a payment and governance token that can be staked to receive frequent rewards!

Governance

APS token holders have the right to participate in governance voting and decision-making to help shape the future of the Pettoverse.

Payment

Players can use the APS token to conduct payments inside the Pettoverse for unlocking the Premium features, territories, and game assets.

Staking

Holders can stake the APS that allows them to earn monthly rewards.
