Alpha Petto Shells

Alpha Petto Shells (APS) are an ERC 20 utility token of the Pettoverse. Holders will
participate in the decision-making of the future of Petto supporting game
evolution and deciding on the allocation of a community treasury.

What is APS→What is APS→What is APS→What is APS→What is APS→ What is APS→ What is APS→ What is APS→ What is APS→

APS is the native token across the Pettoverse. APS can only be earned within the Pettoverse and it’s crucial to the entire game experience and benefits.

APS has a capped total supply and can be exchanged on decentralized marketplaces. Also, APS is a payment and governance token that can be staked to receive frequent rewards!
